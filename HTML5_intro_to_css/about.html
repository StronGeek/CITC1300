Gravelord Nito

Gravelord Nito is one of the main bosses in Dark Souls. One of the original lords of cinder, Nito holds the power over death. They reside in the catacombs, a place where skeletons and necromancers alike call their home, away from the light. When you fight them, it will be in thier own grave. Nito will summon skeletons and use the power of Thier Geavelord Greatsword, as well as death to win. Nito is the easiest of the Four Lords to fight.

Seath the Scaleless

Seath the Scaleless is a dragon that betrayed his own kind. He si desperate for the secrets of immortality. He resides in the Grand Archives, a massive library with plenty of challenging foes.

Bed of Chaos

The Bed of Chaos is the third major boss in Dark Souls. Their place of residence is in Lost Izalith, a place that is home to demons. It is a relust of a failed experiment from the witch of Izalith to recreate the first flame. As a result, this area became the birthplace of demons.

The Four Kings

The four Kings are a group of kings worried about the fall of their kingdom. In their fear, they made a deal with a shady character for protection. However, that deal backfired and they became corrupted by the darkness.

Gwyn, Lord of Cinder

The final boss in the series and Lord of Cinder, Gywn was the first to link the flame and extend the age of fire. He mastered the art of lightning. Once a proud ruler of the kingdom of Anor Londo, he is now a husk of his former self. In an effort to prevent the darkness from taking over he gave his own sanity up. When you meet him, he is hollow and immediately hostile. It is your duty to put him out of his misery and finsih what he started.
