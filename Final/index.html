A Brief History

"The world is changing: I feel it in the water, I feel it in the earth, I smell it in the air." -Galadriel
She couldn't be more right. After an age of peace, war is on the horizion. The dark lord Sauron has returned and is searching for the one ring. Should he find it, all of Middle-Earth will persih. You must journey with different factions on your quest to stop him from regaining the one ring, or, if you have more evil plans, join with the factions actively helping him recover the ring. Whichever side you choose will change the fate of Middle-Earth.
