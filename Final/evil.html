Evil Factions

Feeling evil creeping in. Maybe giving in to temptation to help Sauron. Then you will fit in with the evil factons. These forces are made with one purpose, to destroy the free people of Middle-Earth. With a lot of seige potential, beasts, and numerous hordes at your command, Thr Ring is within your grasp.

Isengard

Should you align with Isengard your foes will cower in fear. With the wizard Saruman and the orc chieftian Lurtz, your enemies will fall. They are the most technologically advanded of the groups, with the abitilty to make explosive mines, ladders, battering rams, and seige balistas. Uruk-hai in heavy armor with forged blades are some of the toughest units in the game. They also make good use of pikemen and crossbowmen.

Mordor

The forces of Mordor are masters of the battlefield. With free orc warriors to recruit they can easily have eyes and axes everywhere. Survive long enough and they can make use of trolls and mumikil. The nazgul on their fellbeast are their heroes. Nothing is more frightening than seeing an army of beasts come after your camp.
