Gondor

It is no question that Gandalf the White is the strongest hero for Gondor. His ability to buff nearby soldiers, use powerful spells like Wizard Blast and Lightning Sword make himm excellent for fighting hordes of enemies as well as doing high single target damage. His level 10 ability, word of power is a huge area of effect (aoe) attack that knocks down any enemy that it does not kill.
